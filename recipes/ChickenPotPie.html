Chicken Pot Pie

Description :

This is a classic recipe for chicken pot pie that has been passed down through my wife's family for several generations. It's an easy recipe to follow, and here's how it's done.

Ingredients :

  • 1 Pie Crust
  • 2 Cups of Cooked Cubed Chicken
  • 1 Cup mixed Potatoes, Peas, Carrots & Corn
  • 1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1/2 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Tbls. Onion Powder
  • A Dash of Poultry Seasoning

Steps :

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Fill pie crust with mixture
  3. Put on the top crust
  4. Bake @ 450 for 15 minutes
  5. Reduce the heat to 350
  6. Bake for 35 more minutes or until golden brown
