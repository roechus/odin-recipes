Meatloaf

Description :

A delicious meatloaf recipe, and here is how it is done!

Ingredients :

  • 2 lbs of hamburg
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups ketchup
  • 1 small onion (cut up small)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps :

  1. Add all ingredients together in a caserole dish
  2. Add a little ketchup on top
  3. Bake @ 350 for about 1 1/2 hours
